About Us

On The Go Car Wash is a Jaipur based organization which provides car cleaning services at Your doorsteps. Our main objective is to make the public get accustomed to a better car cleaning routine, which will ensure a better looking vehicle and prolonged vehicle life.
We are focused on delivering a consistent car wash experience for the customers while ensuring minimal impact on the environment by using only biodegradable chemicals and dry wash techniques. OTG Car Wash experience offers every customer a combination of wash quality, speed and value for the money through its unique wash system with guaranteed satisfaction on every wash. We provide B2B and B2C services by connecting directly with the consumers, societies and corporate hubs and we aim to provide quality services to them. We also put special efforts in providing training to our partners and hence bringing them a sense of empowerment as they become a part of the skilled workforce.
Covid-19 has made us realize the importance of regular cleanliness and sanitization, for that reason, our services are essential in today's time. We are also committed to continuously innovate new ways for our customers to keep their cars looking like new and expand our community leadership through acquisitions, development, and technology.
*Caring for the environment is high on the agenda.

Safety Measures during COVID'19.

At OTG Car Wash, our priority is to keep our partners and customers safe and healthy as it is of utmost importance in today’s risky times.

Accordingly, we have taken all the necessary precautions as a responsible part of this society and we are ready to do our bit. Your car cleaner would go through routine checkups, daily temperature checks, sanitization and would be wearing masks and gloves at all times.

Our service will continue to clean your vehicle using the best biodegradable soaps, polishes, and sealers so that you can have a carefree ride.
